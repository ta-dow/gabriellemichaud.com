Here's a list of items I always bring on camping trips:

  • Nalgenes
  • Jetboil Stove

  • Sponge

  • Microfibre Towel
  • Lighter

  • Toilet Paper
  • Hand Sanitizer
  • Toothbrush
  • Toothpaste

  • Rechargeable Headlamp
  • Portable Charger
  • Charging Cables
  • Carabiners
  • Rope
  • Duct Tape

  • Merino Wool Base Layer
  • Down Jacket
  • Waterproof Outerwear

  • First Aid Kit: Ibuprofen, antihistamines, lip balm, bandages, burn gel, tick removal tweezers, normal saline, hemostatic gauze, water purification tablets.

  • Food: nuts, granola bars, oats, apples, rye bread, rice, dry-cured sausages, ration packs, marinated ribs, halloumi, pizza dough, potatoes.
